RoyaltyLayer

Private royalty splits,
enforced on-chain

Rights-holders register encrypted percentage splits that stay hidden from each other, the platform, and the operator. The contract enforces that splits sum to 100% — without ever seeing the individual values. Revenue is distributed proportionally on encrypted weights. Only you can decrypt your share.

Registration
1 · Register Asset Splits
Actor: Rights Holder / Deal Registrant
idle
Each rights-holder's percentage (in basis points) is encrypted locally before being sent on-chain. The contract never sees individual splits — only encrypted handles. BPS must sum to 10,000 (= 100%). Max 8 stakeholders.
Remaining: 10000 bps
2 · Validate Split Integrity
Actor: Anyone (permissionless)
idle
The KMS (Key Management Service) decrypts only the encrypted sum and returns a cryptographic proof. The contract checks the proof on-chain: if sum = 10,000, the asset activates. Individual splits are never revealed — not even to the KMS.
Revenue & Distribution
3 · Deposit Revenue
Actor: Distributor / Platform
idle
A distributor (streaming platform, sync licensor, label) deposits revenue against the asset. The amount is encrypted before submission — total revenue accumulates as an encrypted on-chain value. No party can read the running total without authorization.
4 · Claim Your Share
Actor: Rights Holder
idle
Each rights-holder claims independently, at any time. The contract computes your entitlement = total revenue × your encrypted split ÷ 10,000 — entirely in FHE, without decrypting either operand. Tokens are transferred as encrypted amounts. No other party sees what you received.
Verification
5 · Decrypt Your Claimed Amount
Actor: Rights Holder (private)
idle
Sign once with your wallet to authorize a user-scoped decryption. The KMS returns your plaintext amount directly to your browser — it never appears on-chain. Other rights-holders and the platform see nothing.

6 · Grant Observer Access
Actor: Rights Holder → Compliance Observer
idle
A rights-holder can voluntarily grant a compliance observer (manager, auditor, label) read access to their encrypted split and claimed total. The grant is permanent in v1 — it cannot be revoked without redeploying the contract. Grant only to trusted parties.
